Reliable partner since 2010

Highview Solutions was founded in Southern California with a clear mission: to simplify international freight for American businesses. Today, we operate from our headquarters in Fontana, CA and major logistics hubs including Los Angeles, Atlanta, Newark, and Dallas.

Our team of licensed freight forwarders, customs brokers, and supply chain specialists work together to deliver end-to-end visibility and operational excellence. We are FF+ IATA accredited and a proud member of the NCBFAA.

Whether you’re shipping automotive parts, consumer goods, or industrial machinery — our tailor-made logistics keep your supply chain fluid. Based in the Inland Empire, we offer unrivaled access to West Coast ports and rail ramps.
